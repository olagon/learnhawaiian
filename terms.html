Accuracy of the vocabulary

We work hard to make every entry correct, with proper ʻokina and kahakō. Hawaiian is a living language with regional variation and ongoing scholarship, so there may be entries you would translate or spell differently. If you spot an error, please let us know.

We are not native speakers of Hawaiian and we do not claim to be. This app is a tool for vocabulary learning. For deeper study, work with a kumu, attend a class, or read works by ʻAha Pūnana Leo, the University of Hawaiʻi Hawaiian Studies department, and other community sources.

The app is provided "as is." We do our best, but we cannot guarantee it will always work, that your progress will never be lost, or that every translation is perfect. Use it as one tool among many in your learning journey.

If for any reason this site goes away, we will give notice on the site and through the email you provided (if you signed in). The data files are open source on GitHub, so the vocabulary itself will outlive any single hosting choice.
